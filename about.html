In Partnership

We operate in partnership with our candidates and clients, to provide unsurpassed recruitment solutions, professionally and at pace. We work as an extension of our client's business, taking time to understand the needs and ethos of each company, to better understand and meet each client’s individual needs.

As an organisation with a Pan European reach, we can extend our services to clients that operate across multiple geographies, servicing their needs, wherever they may be.

Focus

As an organisation we cover all markets, including Telecommunications, Financial Services & Banking, E-Commerce, Retail, Utilities, Defence and others across both Public & Private sectors. The company is structured into divisions, each focusing on individual IT market sectors and technologies.

This ensures knowledge in depth and drives rapid delivery of recruitment services to clients.

Our People

We are proud that all of our people have very extensive experience within their sector. We will not put a graduate in charge of a client account. We do not take a "telesales" approach to building our business.
